About Marwan

Marwan Nassar is a Los Angeles-based musician and educator who has shared stages and studios with Grammy-winning artists, Bootie Brown of The Pharcyde, and Breakestra, performed at SXSW, and brought his work to international audiences. He created Music Alive to help adult musicians close the gap between the music they hear and the music they actually play.
